# Arabic PP-ambiguous test sentences
# Arabic uses prepositional phrases (bi-, fi-, min-, etc.)
# PP can attach to preceding NP (idafa construct) or to the VP

# "I ate the sushi with chopsticks" — bi: instrument (VP) vs attribute (NP)
أكلتُ السوشي بالعصيّ .
akaltu l-sushi bil-3asiyy .

# "She saw the man with a telescope"
رأتْ المرأةُ الرجلَ بالتلسكوب .
ra'at al-mar'atu al-rajula bil-tiliskob .

# "He hit the boy with the stick"
ضربَ الولدَ بالعصا .
daraba al-walada bil-3asa .
