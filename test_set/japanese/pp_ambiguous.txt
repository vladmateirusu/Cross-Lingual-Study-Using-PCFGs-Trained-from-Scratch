# Japanese PP-ambiguous test sentences (postpositional phrases)
# Format: one sentence per line (romanized + original script)
# The postpositional phrase can modify either the verb or the preceding noun

# "I ate sushi with chopsticks" — ohashi-de: instrument (VP) vs accompaniment (NP)
私は 箸で 寿司を 食べた 。
watashi-wa hashi-de sushi-wo tabeta .

# "She saw the man with a telescope"
彼女は 望遠鏡で 男を 見た 。
kanojo-wa bouenkyou-de otoko-wo mita .

# "He hit the boy with the stick"
彼は 棒で 少年を 叩いた 。
kare-wa bou-de shounen-wo tataita .
